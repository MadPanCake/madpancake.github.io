Facility Breach

CO-OP horror in which players have to work together to neutralize an anomaly

Facility Breach tells a story of an abandoned facility. You, and the rest of the Anomaly Team (your friends), have to go inside the building, to investigate the case of an anomaly. Look for the anomaly samples inside the building, in order to analyze them in the laboratorium. Lab's computer gives players useful information about the anomaly's weaknesses, which can later be used to neutralize and contain it.

Game mode features custom 3D models, fully custom-made sounds and a lot of fun for a team of up to 6 friends!

At some point in time, we were playing Phasmophobia with my friends every single day. I really liked the game, although in the beginning, I could barely play it, that's how scared I was. Anyways, at some point I decided to make my own horror, in order to scare my friends!
